About Us

At Coffee House in Marrakech, Morocco, we take pride in being a favorite gathering place for coffee enthusiasts and conversation lovers. Our mission is to provide an exceptional coffee experience in a cozy, welcoming setting where guests can relax, connect, and enjoy every moment in comfort.

Our Menu

  • Capuccino

    A warm embrace in a cup — rich, smooth, and crowned with a cloud of creamy foam , turning every sip into a little moment of joy.

  • Americano

    A refined expression of simplicity smooth, deep, and elegantly balanced, offering the pure essence of coffee in its most graceful form.

  • Espresso

    A bold masterpiece rich, aromatic, and full-bodied. Each sip delivers an awakening of flavor and intensity, crafted for true coffee connoisseurs.

  • Macchiato

    Where strength meets sophistication the intensity of espresso gently softened by a delicate touch of milk, creating a moment of perfect harmony.

  • Frappé

    An indulgent blend of chilled coffee and creamy texture refreshing, silky, and designed to delight with every cool sip.

  • Mocha

    A luxurious fusion of espresso and fine chocolate smooth, velvety, and irresistibly decadent, crafted for those who savor the sweeter side of coffee.
